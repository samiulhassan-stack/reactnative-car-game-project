## ЁЯОо How to Play

### Start Game

2. Game starts immediately

### Controls
- **Swipe Left/Right**: Move car
- **Left/Right Buttons**: Alternative movement
- **Pause Button**: Pause game

### Objectives
- Avoid red enemy cars
- Dodge orange obstacles
- Collect yellow coins
- Pick up blue shields (one-time protection)
- Survive as long as possible
- Beat your high score

### Scoring
- 1 point = per second
- 10 points = per coin
- 50 points = per obstacle avoided
- Bonus = level multiplier

### Game Over
- Collision without shield ends game
- View final stats
- Option to play again or go home
